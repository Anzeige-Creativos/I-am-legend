To take into account


States
Loneliness and Isolation: The central theme is extreme loneliness. The film portrays the devastating psychological effects of prolonged isolation through Neville, who stays sane thanks to his routine, his dog Sam, and his scientific quest. His relationship with Sam is a representation of the human need for companionship. Sam's death is a key emotional turning point, leaving Neville more vulnerable than ever.

Teaching
Survival and Hope: The search for a cure represents hope in the face of a devastated future. Throughout the film, Neville continues to believe in the possibility of reversing the effects of the virus, which motivates him to keep going despite the dire circumstances.

Ideas
The Hero Myth: Neville is presented as a tragic hero, someone willing to sacrifice himself for the greater good. However, his inflexibility and obsession with saving humanity also lead him to despair. The film explores how obsession can be both a driving and destructive force.

Technical Aspects


Performance:
Will Smith gives an emotionally charged performance, being the core of the film. His portrayal of Neville shows the desperation, vulnerability and inner strength of someone who has lost almost everything, resulting in a deep emotional connection with the audience.

Direction and Cinematography:
Francis Lawrence creates a claustrophobic and desolate atmosphere, presenting a post-apocalyptic New York empty of human life. Wide shots of the deserted city emphasize Neville's isolation and the severity of his situation.

Curiosities of the Movie


Based
The film is based on the science fiction novel of the same name written by Richard Matheson and published in 1954. This is the third adaptation of this work, since it previously hit theaters in 1964 as The Last Man on Earth and in 1971 as The Omega Man.

It was necessary
The film presents a bleak vision of New York, with empty streets and nature taking over. To achieve this setting, CGI (computer generated imagery) techniques were used and major streets in the city were closed for several nights to film the scenes.

Shown
Neville's character has a faithful canine companion named Sam. During the filming of I Am Legend, several German Shepherd dogs were used to play Sam due to the length of filming and the different stages of the character's life.

Something you didn't know
In one scene of the film, a promotional poster for a Batman and Superman movie can be seen. This was a nod to I Am Legend director Francis Lawrence. Years later, we were able to see the movie Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016) by Zack Snyder.
