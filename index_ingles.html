I Am Legend

A science fiction thriller from the director of Constantine: I Am Legend. After biological warfare, Robert Neville is the last healthy human being living on Earth, but he is not alone. The other humans have turned into vampire-like mutants and are all hungry for Neville's blood. By day, he is the hunter, stalking the undead that slumber in the abandoned ruins of civilization. At night, he barricades himself in his house and prays for dawn.

"I Am Legend" is a 2007 science fiction thriller film starring Will Smith and directed by Francis Lawrence. It is based on the 1954 novel of the same name by Richard Matheson. The story follows Robert Neville, a military scientist who is seemingly the last human being in New York City after a virus, originally created to cure cancer, has decimated humanity. This virus transforms humans into aggressive nocturnal creatures known as Darkseekers.

Neville spends his days trying to find a cure for the virus, while dealing with the extreme loneliness and despair of living in an empty world. He uses his own immune system as a basis for his experiments, as he is immune to the virus. As the plot progresses, Neville finds signs that there might be other survivors, prompting him to keep fighting to save what's left of humanity. The film explores themes of isolation, sacrifice and the survival of the human species.
